RULES

Intro
WOQ (War Of Questions) is an adventure and strategy role-playing game that is played through questions. Players play as heroes who must explore a fantasy world, fight monsters and progress through the dungeons!

Game’s goal is easy: Players advance in the dungeon by throwing a die and answering questions about the HTML/ CSS theme to defeat enemies.

Depending on the difficulty of the questions, players will be able to do more damage or less to the monsters, defend of not against their attacks.

Players
2

Material
Mobile phone (with internet)

How to play?
Place the paws in the dungeon’s entrance, both players throw the die and the player with the biggest number goes first.

Every player begins at 30 hp. The first player throws the die and move the indicated amount.

If no enemy is encountered, then it’s the second player’s turn.

If there is an encounter (a player if in front of an enemy), then it’s time to fight!

- The player entering the fight starts by choosing an attack damage based on the difficulty of the question they want to try and answer, ranging from 1 to 10.

- Then it’s the monster’s turn, it will pick a random question with a difficulty (damage) rating based on the monster presence (a goblin will have a low damage compared to a big troll or orc).

- Then it’s the other player’s turn, they have a choice, go and fight other enemies or go and help the first player in their fight.

- Then the turns are as followed:

  ° One player battle: Player, Monster, Player, Monster…

  ° Two player battle: Player 1, Player 2, Monster, Player 1, Player 2, Monster…

- Defense :

  ° A player can defend against an enemy’s attack by answering correctly to the question. If answered correctly, the damage will be reduced to 80% to a maximum of 1 damage (except 1 damage questions, they will be completely blocked).

  ° If not answered correctly the full damage will be dealt.

The goal is to defeat all enemies and travel to the end of the dungeon floor.
